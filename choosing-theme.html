4. Choosing a Theme

RetroCraft includes 8 built-in retro themes, each with its own personality:

Theme Vibe Best For
Glitter Y2K Pink, sparkly, early 2000s feminine Beauty blogs, personal shrines, fandom pages
Cyberdeck Terminal Black background, green monospace, hacker aesthetic Tech blogs, coding portfolios, retro gaming
GeoCities Rainbow Bright colors, classic web 1.0 chaos Fan pages, hobby sites, anything maximalist
Soft Kawaii Blog Pastel, rounded, cute and gentle Personal blogs, diaries, cozy content
Scene / Emo Scrapbook Dark, neon accents, angled borders Music pages, alt fashion, scene culture
Minimal Retro Notebook Lined paper, handwriting fonts, clean Journals, reading lists, minimal portfolios
Nail Studio Editorial Burgundy and gold, elegant magazine layout Beauty businesses, portfolios, galleries
Retro Gamer Pixel fonts, 8-bit colors, blocky borders Gaming blogs, walkthrough guides, retro reviews

To switch themes, open the Themes tab in the left sidebar of the Editor and click any theme. The change is instant — no reload needed.
