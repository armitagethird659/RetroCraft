12. 🌍Previewing Your Site

The Preview button in the top bar opens a full-screen preview of your current page, rendered exactly as it will appear when exported. SAVE any changes before you preview, incase any changes have been missed by the 30sec autosave.

What Preview Shows

  • Active theme with all customizations applied (colors, fonts, cursors, backgrounds)
  • All blocks rendered in their final HTML/CSS form
  • Working navigation between pages (click internal links to switch pages in the preview)
  • YouTube embeds and audio players functioning

Switching Pages in Preview

Use the page selector dropdown at the top of the preview to jump between pages, or click navigation links within the preview itself.
⭐!TIP Preview uses the exact same HTML/CSS compilation as the export — so what you see in preview is exactly what you get in the exported site.
