9. Theme Customizer

When no block is selected, the right sidebar shows the Theme Customizer — your control panel for global visual settings.

What You Can Customize

  • Background color — override the theme's default
  • Text color — body text color
  • Heading color — section titles
  • Font family — body and heading fonts (Google Fonts loaded dynamically)
  • Border style — solid, dashed, dotted, double, ridge, outset
  • Border color — applies to panels and dividers
  • Border radius — 0px (sharp) to 20px (rounded)
  • Custom cursor — see section below
  • Background image — see section below
!TIP All customizations are stored as "overrides" on top of the base theme. If you switch themes, your overrides carry over — so your custom colors stay even when you change the overall look.
