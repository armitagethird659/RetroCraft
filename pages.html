5. 📂Managing Pages

Your site is made up of pages. Every project starts with a Home page, and you can add as many as you need.

Adding a Page

  1. In the left sidebar, go to the Pages tab
  2. Click [+ Add Page] at the bottom
  3. Enter the page name (this also generates the URL slug)

Reordering Pages

Drag pages by the ≡ handle on the left of each page row to reorder them. The navigation bar updates automatically to match.

Page Options (right-click or ⋯ menu)

  • Rename — changes the page title and URL slug
  • Duplicate — creates a copy of the page with all its blocks
  • Set as Homepage — marks this page as your index page (shown with 🏠)
  • Delete — removes the page permanently
⭐!TIP Your homepage is always exported as index.html. All other pages use their slug, e.g. about.html, blog.html.
