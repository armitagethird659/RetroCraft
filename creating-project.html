3. Creating a New Site

  1. Click [+ New Site] on the Dashboard
  2. Enter your site name (this becomes your site title — you can change it later)
  3. Browse the theme gallery and click a theme to preview it
  4. Click [Create]

You're now in the Editor. A default "Home" page is created for you automatically.

⭐️!TIP Don't overthink your theme choice — you can switch themes at any time without losing your content. Your blocks and text stay; only the colors and fonts change.
