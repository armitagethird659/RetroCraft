11. 🏞️Background Images

Add a background image to your site for extra atmosphere — perfect for fansites and shrines.

How to Set a Background Image

  1. Open the Theme Customizer (right sidebar, no block selected)
  2. Find the Background Image section
  3. Toggle [Use background image]
  4. Pick an image from your Asset Library or paste a URL
  5. Toggle [Tile] if you want the image to repeat (classic GeoCities style)

Tile vs Cover

  • Tile ON — image repeats to fill the screen (great for patterns, textures, small GIFs). Classic early-web look.
  • Tile OFF — image scales to cover the full screen without repeating. Modern cinematic look.
The background image overrides the theme's background color when enabled. Disable the toggle to revert.
